## Last Updated: 16<sup>th</sup> January, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to LitExplorers.

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle personal information collected through our waitlist sign-up process.

## Information We Collect

- **Email Address:** Provided when you sign up for the waitlist.
- **Name and Interests:** Optionally provided for more personalised communication.

## How We Use Your Information

We will use your email address solely for one notification when the LitExplorers app launches. 

Your name and interests are used to understand our audience better but will not be used for any other purposes without further consent.

## Data Sharing and Third Parties

We use Mailchimp for managing our mailing list. Their privacy policy is available [here](https://www.intuit.com/privacy/statement/).

Please note that your data might be subject to government or agency scrutiny despite our efforts to protect your privacy.

## Server Logs

Our server automatically collects information about your visit, including your IP address, browser type, and access times. These logs are used for maintaining the security and functionality of our site, are rotated every 14 days, and then deleted. 

We do not currently analyse these logs for individual user tracking but might use them in aggregate for site improvement.

## Your Privacy Rights

You can unsubscribe from our mailing list at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any email we send.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page, and where appropriate, we might provide notice of significant changes.
